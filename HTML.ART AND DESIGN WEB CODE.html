About the Department

Established in 1982 as a Women’s Textile College, the Department of Art & Design at University of Azad Jammu & Kashmir has grown into a vibrant hub of creativity and culture.

In 2005, the department expanded to offer a comprehensive Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) program, featuring 8-semester degree courses in Graphic Design, Textile Design, and Painting.

Our focus is a harmonious blend of tradition and modern practices, empowering students to innovate while preserving cultural heritage.

Mission: Empowering students to inspire change, preserve culture, and innovate for the future.

Message from the Head of Department

Dr. Rukhsana Syed Muhammad

At the Department of Art & Design, our vision is to nurture creativity that honors cultural preservation while empowering the next generation of artists and designers. We believe in fostering an environment where tradition meets innovation, and where every student is inspired to contribute meaningfully to the legacy of art and design in our region and beyond.

Our Majors

Graphic Design

Focus on visual communication, typography, publishing, branding, and digital tools to prepare students for dynamic careers in design.

Textile Design

Explore surface pattern design, weaving, textile printing, and fabric technology to innovate in the textile industry.

Painting

Learn classical and modern painting techniques, creativity, studio practices, and participate in exhibitions.

Facilities & Resources

Studios & Workshops

Fully equipped studios and workshops designed for hands-on learning and creative experimentation.

  • Rota print machines, multicolor printers, cutters
  • Cameras, multimedia, and computer services
  • Exhibition spaces for student and faculty showcases

Library & Digital Resources

Access to a rich collection of books, journals, and digital media to support research and learning.

Collaborations & Cultural Engagement

Government Collaborations

We actively participate in exhibitions and awareness campaigns in partnership with government bodies to promote art and culture.
